### Alternatives Details

### 1. Jupiter

- **Strengths**: Anti-MEV execution, Solana DEX aggregation.
- **Weaknesses**: Limited to Solana, no cross-chain support.
- **Best For**: Solana-focused trading bots.

### 2. Birdeye

- **Strengths**: Solana DEX + NFT data, user-friendly API.
- **Weaknesses**: No cross-chain support, limited AI/ML integration.
- **Best For**: NFT traders and Solana analytics.

### 3. Raydium

- **Strengths**: Direct access to Raydium DEX liquidity.
- **Weaknesses**: Limited to Raydium, no advanced analytics.
- **Best For**: Raydium-specific trading strategies.



## Why Choose Mobula API?

### 1. Open and Robust Access

Mobula APIs are open to developers, complete with extensive documentation and SDK support. This openness contrasts sharply with GMGN.ai’s closed system, allowing you to build, test, and scale your applications without restrictions.

### 2. Comprehensive Multi-Chain Data

Unlike alternatives that focus narrowly on one or two blockchains, Mobula supports over 50 chains. Whether you’re targeting Solana, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, or non-EVM networks, Mobula delivers a unified and scalable solution.

### 3. Rich Data Sets and Customization

Mobula doesn’t stop at real-time market data. It offers historical data, detailed token vesting and unlock schedules, and both on-chain and off-chain analytics. This level of granularity and flexibility lets you design custom dashboards, perform in-depth market analysis, and build advanced automated trading strategies.

### 4. Better Developer Experience

With robust documentation, live API references (e.g., [Mobula OpenAPI](https://github.com/MobulaFi/mobula-api-docs/blob/main/rest-api-reference/openapi.yaml)), and active community support, Mobula is built with the developer in mind. The comprehensive tools available streamline integration, ensuring that your trading portal remains agile and feature-rich.
